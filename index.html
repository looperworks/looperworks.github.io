We believe the transition from studio to practice should be navigated with the same rigor and intentionality that defines good design. Threshold equips architecture and landscape architecture students with the strategic tools to present their work, communicate their value, and find firms where they can grow.

This project grew out of years spent on both sides of the profession — practicing architecture, advising students on career strategy, and teaching in the studio. Through those experiences, it became clear that students needed more than a syllabus or a single advising session. They needed an accessible, always-available space where the practical knowledge that usually gets passed down informally — how to build a portfolio, how to write for firms, where the jobs actually are — could be organized, refined, and shared openly.

Threshold is that space.
